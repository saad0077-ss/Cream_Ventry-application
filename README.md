# Creamventory

Creamventory is a Flutter-based inventory management application developed as my first Flutter project. The application helps businesses track products, manage inventory, record sales, and maintain customer information through a simple and user-friendly interface.

## 🚀 Overview

This project was created to gain hands-on experience in Flutter development and understand how real-world business applications are built. During development, I worked with state management, local databases, form validation, CRUD operations, and responsive UI design.

## ✨ Features

- Product Management
  - Add, edit, delete, and view products
  - Track stock quantities
  - Product details management

- Inventory Tracking
  - Monitor available stock
  - Update inventory records
  - Manage product information efficiently

- Sales Management
  - Record sales transactions
  - View sales history
  - Track business performance

- Customer Management
  - Add and manage customer information
  - Maintain customer records

- Dashboard
  - Overview of inventory and sales data
  - Quick access to important business metrics
